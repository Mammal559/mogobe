About Us

Who We Are

Mogobe Inc is a leading real estate investment, development, and property management company based in Gaborone, Botswana. We specialize in owning, developing, and managing a diverse portfolio of commercial, industrial, retail, and residential properties across the country.

Over the years, Mogobe Inc has grown into one of Botswana's notable indigenous real-estate groups, committed to reshaping urban and industrial landscapes through quality developments and sustainable investment.

Our Vision

To become Botswana's most impactful and innovative real-estate developer, transforming communities and enabling economic growth through modern commercial, industrial, and residential infrastructure.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality real estate developments that support business growth and community development
  • Create employment opportunities and empower local entrepreneurs
  • Lead with integrity, innovation, and long-term sustainability
